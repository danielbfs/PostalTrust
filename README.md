## Project Positioning

`Postal Trust` is an open source, self-hosted, and federated platform designed to use permissioned blockchain and native AI for postal traceability, chain of custody, interoperability, and operational intelligence.

`Postal Trust` e uma plataforma open source, self-hosted e federada desenhada para usar blockchain permissionada e IA nativa em rastreabilidade postal, cadeia de custodia, interoperabilidade e inteligencia operacional.

## Contribution Focus

The current repository is focused on open discussion of the white paper, technical architecture, protocol design, privacy model, and AI-native operational workflows.

O foco atual deste repositorio esta na discussao aberta do white paper, da arquitetura tecnica, do desenho do protocolo, do modelo de privacidade e dos fluxos operacionais com IA nativa.
