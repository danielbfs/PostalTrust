# Postal Trust

Open source blockchain infrastructure for postal services and logistics.

## Overview

`Postal Trust` is an open source, self-hosted, and federated platform built to bring auditable traceability, chain of custody, interoperability, and native AI to postal and logistics workflows.

The project combines:

- permissioned blockchain for proof, custody, and auditability;
- native AI for document reading, anomaly detection, reporting, and operational assistance;
- open APIs and protocol design for interoperability;
- privacy-by-design principles for enterprise and regulatory alignment.
